New Providence High School Presents:

In association with
Children with Hair Loss

We are a student-run club dedicated to supporting children with hair loss through charity drives, fundraising events, and awareness campaigns. Our mission is to help kids regain confidence and improve mental health for these children.



We are based on a few principles

  • C - Caring for
  • O - Others'
  • P - Psychological needs with
  • E - Empathy

And we need YOUR help!
